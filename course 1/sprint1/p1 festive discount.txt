Algorithm to Calculate Discount on Total Bill Amount:

1.Take input of total bill amount.
2.If the total bill amount is greater than or equal to $500, then apply the festive discount of 5%.
3.Calculate the discount amount by multiplying the total bill amount by 5%.
4.else the bill amount is lesser than $500 the discount zero.
5.Print the discount amount.

pseudocode


BEGIN
Input total_bill_amount
  begin
      If (total bill amount >= 500)
      discount = total bill amount * 0.05
      Print You have received a festive discount of $  discount   on your total bill.
  end
  begin
      Else
      Print Your total bill is less than $500, so you are not eligible for the festive discount.
  end
END
